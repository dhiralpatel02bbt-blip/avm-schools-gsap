Please note

Admission is subject to vacancy. Once the form is filled, as and when a vacancy arises in the class requested,
the school will evaluate and revert. The decision of the school will be final in all matters.

Incase of issues or queries regarding admissions please email on avm@intelliadmissions.org

Applicants will receive communication as to when they are required to visit the respective schools for
submission and verification of documents.

Parents whose children meet the above criteria for "Early Trail" (Nursery) admission can fill the application
form online and pay a non-refundable application fee of Rs.1000/- through the payment gateway.
