Vishv Hindi Diwas Celebration at Arya Vidya Mandir

INTER-CLASS Bandra (W) • 14.02.2026

"हिंदी है हम सब की पहचान, हिंदी से जुड़े हमा..."

Arya Vidya Mandir, Juhu celebrated Vishv Hindi Diwas with great enthusiasm and linguistic pride on 9th January 2026. The celebration aimed to reinforce the importance of the Hindi language and encourage students to use Hindi confidently in their daily lives.

To mark the occasion, a Hindi Handwriting Competition was conducted for students of the Primary Section (Grades I to IV). The activity provided students with an opportunity to improve their writing skills while appreciating the beauty and structure of the Hindi script. Students participated wholeheartedly, showcasing neatness, clarity, and dedication in their work.

In addition to the competition, students were sensitized to the importance of speaking in Hindi through engaging discussions and age-appropriate informative videos. These visual presentations highlighted Hindi as a unifying language and emphasized its relevance in everyday communication, culture, and national identity.
The entire event was organised by Ms. Pooja Yadav and Ms. Pooja Shroff under the guidance Ms. Srijata Bhattacharya, Primary Subject Head.

The celebration of Vishv Hindi Diwas at Arya Vidya Mandir, Juhu was a successful and enriching experience, inspiring students to take pride in Hindi and encouraging them to use the language with confidence and joy.
