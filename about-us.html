A legacy of timeless values and progressive education.

For over 6 decades, AVM has combined academic rigour with traditional values. What began as a school in Bandra, has grown into a trusted group of schools, shaping generations of confident, grounded learners.

Through changing times, AVM's core purpose has remained constant: to develop not only the intellect, but also character, self-awareness and responsibility towards society.

Our Vision

To become the exemplary Institution that develops future ready, successful, happy individuals grounded in human values

AVM Core Values

Inspired from the 10 Arya Samaj principles.

The pursuit of truth and knowledge

Truth is discovered through curiosity, openness, and the willingness to question what we know.

Learning as the essence of life

Learning is a journey sustained by curiosity, shaped by dialogue, and carried forward through continuous exploration.

Clarity of thought

To think clearly is to question, evaluate, and build understanding with purpose.

A commitment to diversity and inclusion

AVM is an inclusive learning community. Students from varied backgrounds, abilities and perspectives are welcomed and supported. Respect for difference is actively nurtured, and every child is encouraged to find their voice and place within the school community.

Governance and transparency

AVM is guided by strong governance practices that ensure clarity, accountability and ethical conduct. Clear policies, structured leadership and transparent communication with parents and stakeholders help maintain trust and consistency across all campuses.
